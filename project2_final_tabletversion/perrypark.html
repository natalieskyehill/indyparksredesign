Park History
Through community and business partnerships, Perry Park is making a difference. Established in 1961 Perry Park is the only current Indy Parks ice rink. It hosts The Perry South Stars High School hockey team, South Indy Youth Hockey league, Sycamore Figure Skating Club, and Elite Skating at the ice rink. The park had a remodel inside during January 2013 with the help of Citizens Energy. Perry Park Aquatic Center was remodeled in the 90’s and received upgrades like our 2 big waterslides.

FAQs
What is your ice rink season?
The ice rink typically opens up the first weekend of October and that goes until the third weekend of March.
Can we bring outside food into the pool or ice rink?
No. We have a concession stand, so we don’t allow any outside food into our facilities.

Park Features
Perry Park boasts an NHL sized ice rink with 4 locker rooms. We also a have concession stand that is open daily during ice rink and aquatic season. Outside Perry Park has 2 shelters, basketball court, tennis courts, and playground.
The ice rink is available to rent during the rink season upon request.

Aquatic Features
Perry Park Aquatic Center will open on May 27th. We are open Mon, Tues, Thurs, and Fri 11-6PM, Wed and Sat 11-8PM, and Sun 12-6PM.
Perry Park Aquatic Center has 2 large water slides including a tube slide for our patrons 48 inches or taller. It also hosts a smaller Polar Bear slide and play area and slide for the younger children. The pool has a zero depth entry.
We have a concession stand and lawn chairs for our guests too.
